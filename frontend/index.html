Your Personalised Menstrual Health Planner

Menstrual cycles affect women in more ways than we think. The changes in each phase require special attention in diet and exercise. These practices aren't widely adopted.
We aim to provide users with personalised guidance on diet and exercise plans to follow. This is designed by determining what phase of the cycle you are currently in.
